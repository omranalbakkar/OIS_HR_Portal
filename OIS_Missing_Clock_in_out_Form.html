How to Submit Missing Records

  1. Fill in your details in the Submitter Information section
  2. Add missing entries using the "Add New Entry" button
  3. Required fields: Employee Code, Name, Project Code, Date, Type, Time, and Reason
  4. Format:
    • Employee Code: E5704
    • Project Code: e.g. LS00001, GC00001, DM00001, CN00001 or 200101
    • Time: Use time picker or enter as HH:MM (24-hour format)
    • Reason: Choose from dropdown. If "Others" selected, additional remarks required
  5. Submit for HR review
Note: All submissions are reviewed by HR. False information may result in disciplinary action.
